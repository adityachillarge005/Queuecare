How QueueCare Simplifies Flow

A structured three-step approach to managing waiting lines digitalized.

1. Add & Select Patient

Staff registers a patient in the system, or patients can register themselves online to quickly link accounts.

2. Auto-Generate Token

Book an appointment for a specific doctor and date. The system automatically issues a sequential daily token number.

3. Manage Live Queue

Clinic staff calls next, updates status, and completes visits. Patients monitor progress and remaining queue size live.

For Clinics & Doctors

  • Organized Patient Flow Maintain distinct queues per doctor and clear waiting lists sorted by tokens.
  • Consolidated Administration Dashboard Real-time statistics including waiting counts, serving numbers, and daily completions.
  • Instant Call Next Actions Transition waiting patients to consultation rooms with one-click flow controls.

For Patients

  • Eliminate Waiting Room Anxiety Patients know their exact token number and their position in the queue.
  • Live Queue Status Tracking Calculate the count of patients ahead on the patient portal in real time.
  • Appointment History Look back at past clinic visits, specialized doctors, and previous token records.
